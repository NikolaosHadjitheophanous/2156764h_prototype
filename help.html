Two factor Authentication
              What is ImQuest ?                                          

              ImQuest is a novel technique that uses two factors for authentication. One factor is an image and the second factor is a challenge question. In this experiment we wish to measure the usability of both these factors in conjunction.                                          


              Registration                                          

              Step1: Enter your basic details. Once your details have been entered the username is validated to check if the already exists or not. If the username exists you will be alerted, else you can proceed to the next step.                                          
              Step2: Choose four challenge questions. You can either choose questions from the database or give your own unique questions. Once the answers have been provided and submitted you proceed to the final step.                                          
              Step 3: Choose an image. You can refresh images by clicking on the refresh button. In order to select an image simply click on it. You can also zoom in and zoom out the image using your mouse.                                          
             

Authentication 		                                         

              Step 1: Enter your username and submit it.                                          
              Step 2: One question out of the four answered will be presented. Once you have answered the question and submitted it you will be taken to the next step.                                          
              Step3: You have to select the image corresponding to your account theta you had chosen during registration.. If you are successful you will be taken to the respective home page.
